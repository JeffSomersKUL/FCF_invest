How do we work?

To ensure seamless operations within our investment fund, we have implemented a series of measures. Firstly, we distinguish our members based on their level of commitment and willingness to participate in discussions. Accordingly, we provide tailored types of meetings. In addition to the meetings, we utilize various communication platforms. Lastly, we provide weekly updates to our portfolio to keep our members informed and enable them to track their investment progress.

Members

We categorize our fund into two distinct types of members, passive and active.

Passive members are not obligated to participate in the partnership and have the flexibility to learn at their own pace. However, this designation also results in a relatively reduced influence on the investing strategy.

Active members are highly engaged in the markets and engage in daily discussions on positions and market developments. As an active member, you are expected to vote on new positions, which requires a level of commitment and contribution.

It is important to note that members have the flexibility to switch between these roles based on their interests and preferences, if the requirements are met.

Meetings

Like our members, we divide our meetings into two types.

Investor meetings are led by the board or active members and prioritize learning. They offer interactive sessions, covering macroeconomic developments and in-depth presentations on relevant topics. These sessions also include a review of the fund's portfolio, allowing members to gain valuable insights.

Active meetings offer a more relaxed and less structured setting. Market movements and new investment opportunities are discussed, providing a platform for members to share their ideas and receive feedback.

Communication

Discord is our primary communication tool, it serves as the hub for daily updates and interactions. Members can expect daily briefings on macroeconomics, technical analyses, trades, portfolio updates and learning resources, along with participation in our online meetings.

WhatsApp complements our communication strategy by alerting members to upcoming presentations and events. This platform ensures timely notifications so everyone can seamlessly participate in all our club activities.

Portfolio

Explore our weekly portfolio updates, where we transparently present our current holdings, profit figures for each position, and in-depth analysis of performance. Gain valuable insights with comparisons to most popular benchmarks, ensuring you stay informed and confident in our investment journey.
