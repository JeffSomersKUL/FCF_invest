How do we work?
To ensure seamless operations within our investment club, we have implemented a series of measures. Firstly, we distinguish our members based on their level of commitment and willingness to participate in discussions. Accordingly, we provide tailored types of presentations. In addition to the presentations, we utilize Discord as our primary communication platform, with support from Whatsapp and Drive. This enables effective communication and collaboration among our members. Lastly, we provide weekly updates to our portfolio to keep our members informed and enable them to track their investment progress.
Members
We categorize our club into two distinct groups, passive and active members
Passive members are not obligated to participate in the partnership and have the flexibility to learn at their own pace. However, this designation also results in a relatively reduced number of voting rights.
Active members, on the other hand, are highly engaged in the markets and engage in daily discussions on positions and market developments. As an active member, you are expected to vote on new positions, which requires a level of commitment and contribution.
It is important to note that members have the flexibility to switch between these roles based on their interests and preferences.
Presentations
Like our members, we divide our presentations into two types.
Active Presentations are led by the board or active members and prioritize learning. They offer interactive sessions, covering macroeconomic developments and in-depth presentations on relevant topics. These sessions also include a review of the club's portfolio, allowing members to gain valuable insights.
Passive Presentations offer a more relaxed and less structured setting. These sessions are open to pitches for new shares, providing a platform for members to share their investment ideas and receive feedback. Discussions about the previous week's market movements are encouraged, making these presentations particularly beneficial for active members engaged in trading.
