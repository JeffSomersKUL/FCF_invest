How do we work?
To ensure seamless operations within our investment club, we have implemented a series of measures. Firstly, we distinguish our members based on their level of commitment and willingness to participate in discussions. Accordingly, we provide tailored types of presentations on a weekly rotating basis. In addition to the presentations, we utilize Discord as our primary communication platform, with support from Messenger, Drive, and email. This enables effective communication and collaboration among our members.
