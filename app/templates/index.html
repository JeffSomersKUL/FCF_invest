How do we work?
To ensure seamless operations within our investment club, we have implemented a series of measures. Firstly, we distinguish our members based on their level of commitment and willingness to participate in discussions. Accordingly, we provide tailored types of presentations on a weekly rotating basis. In addition to the presentations, we utilize Discord as our primary communication platform, with support from Messenger and Drive. This enables effective communication and collaboration among our members. Lastly, we provide weekly updates to our portfolio to keep our members informed and enable them to track their investment progress.
Members
We categorize our members into two distinct groups:
  • passive
  • active
Passive members are not obligated to participate in the partnership and have the flexibility to learn at their own pace. However, this designation also results in a relatively reduced number of voting rights.
Active members, on the other hand, are highly engaged in the markets and engage in daily discussions on positions and market developments. As an active member, you are expected to vote on new positions, which requires a level of commitment and contribution. It is important to note that members have the flexibility to switch between these roles based on their interests and preferences.
