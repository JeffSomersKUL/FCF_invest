How do we work?
To ensure seamless operations within our investment club, we have implemented a series of measures. Firstly, we distinguish our members based on their level of commitment and willingness to participate in discussions. Accordingly, we provide tailored types of presentations on a weekly rotating basis. In addition to the presentations, we utilize Discord as our primary communication platform, with support from Messenger, Drive, and email. This enables effective communication and collaboration among our members. Lastly, we provide weekly updates to our portfolio to keep our members informed and enable them to track their investment progress.
Members
We categorize our members into two distinct groups:
  • passive
  • active
Passive members are not obligated to participate in the partnership and have the flexibility to learn at their own pace. However, this designation also results in a relatively reduced number of voting rights.
Active members, on the other hand, are highly engaged in the markets and engage in daily discussions on positions and market developments. As an active member, you are expected to vote on new positions, which requires a level of commitment and contribution. It is important to note that members have the flexibility to switch between these roles based on their interests and preferences.
Presentations
Our club hosts two different types of presentations: passive meetings and active meetings.
  • passive
  • active
Passive meetings are led by the board or active members and focus on learning. These interactive sessions begin with an introduction and a summary of macroeconomic developments, followed by a presentation on a relevant topic, such as a stock or industry trend. The session then progresses to a review of the club's portfolio, and concludes with an after-dinner discussion.
Active meetings, on the other hand, are less structured and allow for pitches to be made for new shares, as well as discussions on the previous week's market movements. These meetings are geared more towards the active members of the club who are actively engaged in trading.
By offering both types of presentations, our investment club ensures that all members have opportunities to learn and contribute in their own unique ways.
